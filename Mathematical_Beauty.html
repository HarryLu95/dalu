Mathematical Beauty

Archimedean Spiral

\[x=(\alpha+\beta\theta)\cos\theta\] \[y=(\alpha+\beta\theta)\sin\theta\] Take \(\alpha=0\) and \(\beta=1\)

Logarithmic Spirals

\[x=\alpha e^{\beta\theta}\cos\theta\] \[y=\alpha e^{\beta\theta}\sin\theta\] Take \(\alpha=0.1\) and \(\beta=0.1\)

A special case: when \(\beta\approx0.3063489\), the spiral is called a Golden Spiral.
